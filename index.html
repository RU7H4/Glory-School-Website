About Glory School

Yodit Meket’s journey is one of resilience, passion, and vision. In 2004, when many might have thought starting a new venture while expecting a child was too much, Yodit embarked on a bold path to create a school from scratch. While pregnant, she laid the foundation of Glory School, driven by a dream to provide quality education to her community.

  • Number of Staff: More than 200 staff
  • Number of Students: Over 1,400 students across more than three branches.
  • Branches: Megnagia, Lemhotel, Gerji.

Extra Curricular Activities

Glory School offers extra-curricular activities to its students. Extra-curricular learning refers to educational activities that take place outside of the traditional classroom setting.

School Trips

Glory School is committed to developing a range of school trips that enhance the learning experience of our students. School trips provide students with valuable learning experiences outside of the classroom.

Sports

Sport is an important aspect of education at Glory School Ethiopia. We recognize that sport offers a range of physical, social, and emotional benefits for students. We offer a range of different sports, allowing students to participate in activities that they enjoy and that suit their individual interests and abilities.
